Becoming a Saudi ARAMCO supplier.

Learn how to become a supplier for Saudi ARAMCO, the world's largest oil company. Acquiring an ARAMCO vendor number and qualification is essential for companies aspiring to provide products or services to this industry giant.

How to become a supplier for Saudi ARAMCO?

To supply products or services to Saudi Aramco, you must have a vendor number and may need to qualify your offerings (products or services). All these requirements are put in place to ensure suppliers have what it takes to provide best-in-class products and services to Saudi Aramco.

ARAMCO Vendor number and Qualification

With a priority towards continuously improving their business activities and operations, Saudi Aramco have a careful selection process for those wishing to become approved suppliers. Due to the nature of the industry, it is expected that suppliers meet the highest international standards in products, services, and quality control through a rigorous selection process.

How to become a supplier for Saudi ARAMCO?

To secure a position as a Saudi ARAMCO supplier, meeting stringent requirements and achieving top-notch standards in products, services, and quality control is crucial. The rigorous selection process underscores Saudi Aramco's commitment to excellence, ensuring only the best-in-class suppliers are entrusted with their operations.
